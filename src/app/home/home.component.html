AIHacks4Good

Virtual Event, Oct 1-2, 2022

Join us while we tackle some of the biggest challenges in Ethical AI such as:

AI and data usage in healthcare

  • Medical knowledge + treatment is biased
  • Models based on biased human data
  • How can AI models account for this?

Automated Facial Recognition

  • Data is not inclusive of users
  • Errors more likely for non-white + non-male users
  • How can this be corrected? How can we debias automated facial recognition (interdisciplinary approach)

AI and data usage in the justice system

  • AI in the criminal justice system is used as a prevention and prediction tool
  • video surveillance analysis to detect suspicious activities to algorithms
  • Used to predict the likelihood of recidivism and recommend parole
  • AI built on biases
